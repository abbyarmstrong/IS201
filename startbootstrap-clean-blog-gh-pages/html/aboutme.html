I was born in a small town in Nebraska, when I was young my family moved to Utah. After about 10 years in Utah we moved to Rexburg Idaho. We have been there for about 10 years. I love the outdoors. I enjoy skiing, hiking, and rafting. Summer in Idaho is my favorite. When the weather is bad, I like to stay inside and bake.

Chocolate chip cookies are one of my favorite things to bake!

I am currently a student at Brigham Young University. It was always a dream of mine to attend BYU, and I feel very lucky to be a student here. I hope to study accounting, and I am applying to the program this spring. While at BYU I have had the oppertunity to make many wonderful friends and learn MUCH more than I had ever dreamed.
